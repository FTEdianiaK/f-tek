Launcher for mod. Minecraft

A newer version of the older PolyMC, which has seperated from MultiMC.
Allows the downloading of mods and modpacks from Modrinth and CurseForge.

Server console

Switched to it from Minehut after they (partially) pay-walled it.
More exist but this one suits me the most.

Minehut
